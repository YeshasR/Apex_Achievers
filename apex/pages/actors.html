Here's a rundown of top 3 Actors of all time!

Leonardo DiCaprio

November 11, 1974

Everyone's favorite Titanic man is impossible to forget once you see his performance. He is known for having one of the most diverse careers in the world, playing in low-budget films and high-budget films and supporting roles in many movies. He has won 3 Golden Globe Awards and he won an Oscar for his film The Revenant (2015) and has performed in one of history's most iconic films - Titanic (1997).

Anupam Kher

March 7, 1955

One of the most respected and highly regarded actors from India. Anupam Kher has made an irreplaceable mark in Hindi cinema. He made his acting debut in 1982 with the movie Aagman. He has bagged a lot of Filmfare Awards, along with some National Awards, International Indian Film Academy Awards, Screen Actors Guild Awards, and many more.

Christian Bale

January 30, 1974

He is one of the youngest actors to catch the interest of the film audience. He was 13 years old when he got a role in Empire of the Sun (1987). He gained immense popularity when he became known as Bruce Wayne or Batman in the movie Batman Begins (2005). Batman Begins (2005), The Dark Knight (2008), and The Dark Knight Rises (2012). He won several reputed accolades like the Academy Awards, Golden Globe, and Screen Actors Guild Awards
